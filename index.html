about us

Welcome To Aliments Kitchen Restaurant

We are located in Edmonton and have been serving food, drinks and hospitality for years. We have established Aliments Kitchen and gained popularity for its friendly atmosphere, comfort food and reasonable pricing. If you are looking to enjoy sports night, then come on over to Aliments Kitchen to enjoy games on big screens and game atmosphere at its best. So whether you are looking to enjoy a game night, or ladies night out or just eat out, then come on over for an experience you will not forget!


Visit our bar to experience our relaxing atmosphere, to find peace of mind and enjoy our diverse and delicious gourmet menu.
